Coffee Scrub — the "Zig-Zag Frosting" pour

Squeeze the Coffee Body Scrub refill pouch in one continuous, thick rope while slowly moving your hand from side to side across the jar. It should look like frosting being piped onto a cake, creating clean and satisfying ridges.

Please

  • Keep the nozzle close to the surface
  • Pour slowly and consistently
  • Clearly capture the thick coffee scrub texture
  • Avoid squeezing the product into one messy pile
