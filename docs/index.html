The vibe & the golden rules

Talk like her, not like a brand

The vibe: for the girls who want that effortless, rich-girl-on-vacation glow — without a high-maintenance 10-step routine. Real-talk, a little unhinged, very online.

✅ Do say

  • "glazed donut skin", "de-crusting", "girl math", "sustainable baddie"
  • "no gatekeeping", "the girl math is mathing", "top-tier"
  • Talk about strawberry legs, patchy fake tan, waterproof SPF, pool chlorine
  • Sound like you're texting your best friend
  • Be specific & sensory — "thick like cake frosting", "reflects the sunlight"

🚫 Don't say

  • "anti-aging", "best-seller", "clinically proven"
  • Any corporate / infomercial voice
  • Over-scripted, word-perfect delivery
  • Long product monologues — keep it snappy
  • Don't oversell — let the texture & glow do the talking

Video 1 · Aesthetic / Music-led

The "De-Crusting" ASMR Restock

Super fast-paced, highly satisfying visual ASMR restock. Refill your old jars from the 20oz pouches. No talking — a trending sped-up / lo-fi track layered with crisp real ASMR (pouch crinkle, thick pour, jar tap). Target length ~8–12s.

On-screen text hooks — drop one at exactly 0:00

"My toxic trait is buying body care in 20oz bulk because I refuse to have crusty skin this summer 💀"

"Stop buying new plastic jars every month. This 20oz refill hack is top-tier girl math ✨"

"De-crusting my skin after a 3-day pool party. The texture is literal cake frosting."

Shot-by-shot flow
0–3s
The Squeeze

Tight macro shot of your hands squeezing the Coffee Scrub refill in one continuous, thick rope. Move in slow zig-zag lines so it builds clean, satisfying ridges inside the jar — exactly like piping frosting on a cake.

3–6s
The Aloe Pour

Cut to squeezing the clear, juicy, instantly-cooling Aloe Gel refill into its jar. Let it catch the light so the gel looks glossy and alive.

6–10s
The Payoff

Split-second of the scrub melting on wet skin in the shower → fast transition to the Aloe Gel swiped onto your legs in direct sunlight, leaving a crazy reflective, glazed sheen. End on the glow.
